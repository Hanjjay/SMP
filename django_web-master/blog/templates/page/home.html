  3. Corona map

    4. This chapter describes the results of Power Spectral Density (PSD) values obtained from signal analysis and body composition results. The results of PSD consist of graph of average PSD values for alpha band in EEG signal obtained from each subjects for each cycle. Bar graph is also presented in this part to compare average PSD values in alpha band before and after squatting. The results of body composition composed of body capacitance, resistance, reactance and phase angle. The phase angles are compared before and after squatting and the difference between them is calculated.
